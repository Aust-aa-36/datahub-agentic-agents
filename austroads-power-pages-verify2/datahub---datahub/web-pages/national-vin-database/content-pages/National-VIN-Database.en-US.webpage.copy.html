National VIN Database

The central national repository of Vehicle Identification Numbers — maintaining ADR-compliant 17-character VINs for all vehicles manufactured from 1989 onwards.

What it is

The National VIN Database is the authoritative central repository of Vehicle Identification Numbers held within NEVDIS. It stores the 17-character VIN for every vehicle manufactured from 1989 onwards. Manufacturers and their agents submit VINs to NEVDIS before vehicles are registered, enabling cross-referencing across all Australian jurisdictions.

What it contains

Each VIN is a 17-character code compliant with ISO 3780. The first three characters form the World Manufacturer Identifier (WMI), issued by SAE under ISO 3780, identifying the country of manufacture and the specific manufacturer. NEVDIS issues and maintains WMIs for vehicles registered in Australia.

Example VIN: 1HGBH41JXMN109186 — where 1HG is the WMI (Honda, USA), BH41J is the Vehicle Descriptor Section, and XMN109186 is the Vehicle Identifier Section.

Data quality

Uniqueness checks are applied to all submitted VINs. NEVDIS conducts compliance audits against ADR requirements. VINs submitted by batch file or manual entry (up to 5 per day) are validated before being accepted into the database.

Terms of use

  • Information sourced from manufacturers, agents, and state/territory road agencies
  • Data provided 'as is' without guarantee of accuracy or completeness
  • Coverage limited to vehicles manufactured 1989 and later
  • Use limited to verification purposes — not proof of legal ownership

How data flows

  1. Manufacturer authorises a VIN submitter (agent or themselves) to submit VINs on their behalf.
  2. VIN submitter applies for accreditation with NEVDIS, including WMI allocation if needed.
  3. Accredited submitter uploads VINs to NEVDIS via batch file or manual entry (up to 5/day).
  4. NEVDIS validates VINs and passes to ROVER (the national registration of vehicles system).
  5. State and territory registration authorities access VINs from ROVER to support vehicle registration.
