Smart OBM Data Services

Statistical analysis of Smart On-Board Mass data for NTF-enrolled vehicles — throughput and mass insights across road corridors for infrastructure safety and compliance.

What it does

Smart OBM (On-Board Mass) Data Services aggregate mass data from Smart OBM-equipped vehicles enrolled in the National Telematics Framework. The service provides statistical insights into the load profiles of heavy vehicles across road corridors — supporting infrastructure safety, mass compliance analysis, and pavement management decisions.

Who uses it

Road authorities assessing pavement impact, heavy vehicle regulators monitoring mass compliance, infrastructure planners prioritising pavement maintenance, and researchers studying freight loading patterns.

How it works

  1. Smart OBM devices measure vehicle mass at the axle level in real time while the vehicle is operating.
  2. Mass readings are combined with spatiotemporal position data to link loads to specific road segments.
  3. Data is aggregated by corridor, time period, and vehicle class to produce statistical summaries.
  4. Outputs are delivered via ArcGIS dashboards and analytical reports.

Terms of use

  • Mass data aggregated and anonymised — no individual vehicle or operator identification
  • Represents NTF-enrolled Smart OBM vehicles only — not the full heavy vehicle fleet
  • Not to be used for enforcement purposes against specific operators
  • Governed by TAP Terms and Conditions
