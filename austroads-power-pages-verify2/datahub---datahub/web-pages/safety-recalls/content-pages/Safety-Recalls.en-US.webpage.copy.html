Safety Recalls

Real-time lookup matching manufacturer safety recall notices against registered VINs — protecting consumers and enabling compliance checking across the vehicle fleet.

What it does

The Safety Recalls product matches manufacturer-issued safety recall notices against vehicle VINs held in NEVDIS. Users can query by VIN to determine whether a specific vehicle is subject to an outstanding safety recall, enabling action before the vehicle is driven, sold, or serviced.

Who uses it

Consumers verifying a pre-purchase vehicle, car dealers conducting pre-sale safety checks, fleet managers reviewing vehicles for outstanding recalls, insurers assessing liability exposure, and regulators monitoring recall compliance rates.

Use cases

Pre-purchase safety verification, dealer pre-delivery inspection, fleet recall management, ACCC recall compliance checking, and roadside enforcement of recall obligations.

Inputs

InputTypeDescription
VINString17-character Vehicle Identification Number to check

Outputs

OutputTypeDescription
Recall StatusBooleanWhether the VIN is subject to an active recall
Recall ReferenceStringACCC recall reference number if applicable
Recall DescriptionStringSummary of the safety issue and required remedy
Manufacturer ContactStringContact details for recall action

Process

  1. Manufacturer lodges a recall notice with ACCC and NEVDIS, including a list of affected VINs.
  2. NEVDIS stores the recall notice and associates it with affected VINs in the national database.
  3. User or system queries NEVDIS with a VIN to check recall status.
  4. NEVDIS returns current recall status — matched against active recall records in real time.

Terms of use

  • Recall data sourced from ACCC and manufacturer notifications
  • A 'no recall found' result does not mean the vehicle is free from defects
  • Data provided 'as is' without guarantee of completeness
  • Recall status may change — check regularly for vehicles under active management
