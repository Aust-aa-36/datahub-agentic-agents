National Exchange of Vehicle & Driver Information

NEVDIS

Australia's authoritative national system for vehicle and driver information — enabling secure, real-time data exchange between road transport authorities, law enforcement, and authorised entities across all jurisdictions.

Data Elements

Foundation

The specific data components held within NEVDIS — authoritative records that form the building blocks of all services and products.

🔗

Registration status, expiry, jurisdiction, and registered operator details for all vehicles across Australia.
