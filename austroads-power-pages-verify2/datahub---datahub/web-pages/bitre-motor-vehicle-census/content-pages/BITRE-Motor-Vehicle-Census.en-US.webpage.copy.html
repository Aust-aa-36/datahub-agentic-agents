BITRE Motor Vehicle Census

Aggregated vehicle data supplied to BITRE for producing Australia's official national Motor Vehicle Census — the authoritative annual count of registered vehicles.

What it does

NEVDIS supplies aggregated and de-identified vehicle registration data to the Bureau of Infrastructure and Transport Research Economics (BITRE) on an annual basis. BITRE uses this data to produce the national Motor Vehicle Census — Australia's authoritative count of registered vehicles published annually.

Who uses it

BITRE is the sole recipient. The resulting Motor Vehicle Census publication is used by governments, researchers, transport planners, and industry for fleet composition analysis, emissions estimation, and infrastructure planning.

Process

  1. At the agreed annual census date, NEVDIS extracts a snapshot of registered vehicle records across all jurisdictions.
  2. Records are aggregated and de-identified — no individual owner information is included.
  3. BITRE receives the aggregated dataset and uses it to produce the Motor Vehicle Census publication.
  4. The Motor Vehicle Census is published publicly by BITRE.

Terms of use

  • Governed by a formal data sharing agreement between Austroads and BITRE
  • Access restricted to BITRE for census production purposes only
  • All individual records are aggregated and de-identified before supply
