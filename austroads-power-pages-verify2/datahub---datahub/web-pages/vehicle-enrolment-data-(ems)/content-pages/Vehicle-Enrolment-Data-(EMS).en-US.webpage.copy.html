Vehicle Enrolment Data (EMS)

Core registration and identity data for heavy vehicles enrolled in the National Telematics Framework — linking vehicles, telematics devices, jurisdictions, and operators.

What it is

The Enrolment Management System (EMS) holds the core identity and registration data for every heavy vehicle enrolled in TCA's National Telematics Framework (NTF). It links the vehicle to its telematics device, operator, jurisdictional registration, and service provider — forming the authoritative registry underpinning all TCA compliance and analytics services.

What it contains

Vehicle identity (VIN, registration plate, class), telematics device details (device ID, service provider), operator and accreditation information, enrolment status, and jurisdictional links.

Example uses

Vehicle identity verification for compliance, linking telematics data to specific vehicles, operator accreditation checking, and jurisdictional oversight of enrolled vehicles.

Terms of use

  • Governed by the National Telematics Framework and relevant state-based legislation
  • Access restricted to authorised jurisdictions, TCA, and accredited service providers
  • Used solely for compliance, safety, and authorised analytics purposes

Access process

  1. Operator applies to enrol their heavy vehicle(s) via their jurisdiction's NTF enrolment process.
  2. Telematics service provider fits and activates an approved device.
  3. TCA records the enrolment in EMS, linking vehicle, device, operator, and jurisdiction.
  4. Enrolment data is accessible to authorised parties via TCA's data systems.
