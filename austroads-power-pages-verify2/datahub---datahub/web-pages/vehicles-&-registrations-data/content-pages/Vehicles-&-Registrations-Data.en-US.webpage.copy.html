Vehicles & Registrations Data

Comprehensive vehicle and registration records from all Australian states and territories — the foundational dataset for identity verification, compliance checking, and fleet management.

What it is

NEVDIS is a secure national system that brings together vehicle registration records from all Australian states and territories. It stores essential information about vehicles, their registrations, transfer history, and applicable conditions — enabling real-time, secure data sharing between road transport authorities, law enforcement, and authorised entities.

What it contains

The dataset broadly covers four groups: Vehicle Details (make, model, body type, engine, key specifications), Heavy Vehicle Details (GVM, GCM, axle configuration), High-Performance Vehicle Details (power output, power-to-weight ratio), and Registration Details (plate, validity period, conditions, operator details, and transfer history).

Data quality

Records are supplied directly by state and territory road authorities. Quality is maintained through manual audits, automated privacy compliance checks, cross-verification with government registries, and automated validation against NEVDIS and manufacturer data. Updates are applied in real time as jurisdictions report changes.

Example uses

Ownership and identity verification, fraud detection, VIN validation, compliance checking and reporting, registration renewal reminders, insurance risk assessment, fleet management, and operational analytics.

Terms of use

  • Information sourced from state/territory road agencies and police
  • Data provided 'as is' without guarantee of accuracy or completeness
  • No liability for errors, omissions, or reliance on NEVDIS data
  • Use limited to verification purposes — not proof of legal ownership
  • Coverage may be incomplete; absence of data does not imply clearance

How data flows

  1. When a jurisdiction issues a new registration, it shares the details with NEVDIS.
  2. When registration details or status changes, the jurisdiction shares updates with NEVDIS.
  3. When a vehicle transfers between jurisdictions, the new registration details are shared with NEVDIS.
  4. Other jurisdictions refer to NEVDIS to access current vehicle registration details.
