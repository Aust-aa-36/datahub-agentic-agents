Road Analytics Services

Aggregated analysis of heavy vehicle road usage across different states — insight into how monitored vehicles use road corridors over time.

What it does

TCA's Road Analytics Services aggregate spatiotemporal telematics data from NTF-enrolled heavy vehicles to produce corridor-level road usage insights. These services reveal how, when, and how frequently monitored vehicles use specific road segments — supporting infrastructure planning, road management, and investment prioritisation.

Who uses it

Road authorities and infrastructure planners, state and territory government transport agencies, freight policy developers, and research organisations. Data is available for all states across the national road network.

How it works

  1. Spatiotemporal records from enrolled heavy vehicles are map-matched to the road network segments.
  2. Matched records are aggregated by road segment, time period, and vehicle class.
  3. Aggregated metrics (vehicle counts, speeds, usage frequencies) are computed per corridor.
  4. Results are published to ArcGIS dashboards and available as bulk dataset extracts.

Terms of use

  • All data aggregated and anonymised — no individual vehicle identification possible
  • Metrics derived from NTF-enrolled vehicles only — not representative of total freight task
  • Governed by TAP Terms and Conditions

Access process

  1. Submit access request via TCA TAP User Enrolment.
  2. Eligibility and privacy checks completed.
  3. Access provisioned to appropriate ArcGIS dashboards. Average approval: 2 business days.
