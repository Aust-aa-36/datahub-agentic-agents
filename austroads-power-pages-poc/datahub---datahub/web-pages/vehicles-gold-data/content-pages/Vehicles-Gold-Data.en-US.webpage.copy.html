Vehicles Gold Data

Manufacturer-supplied vehicle specifications from FCAI/VFACTS — the single source of truth for vehicle identification, enabling accurate registration and compliance matching across jurisdictions.

What it is

Vehicles Gold Data is the authoritative manufacturer-supplied dataset of vehicle specifications held within NEVDIS. It is sourced from the Federal Chamber of Automotive Industries (FCAI) via their VFACTS program, now operated by S&P Global. This data represents the 'gold standard' for vehicle identity and specification matching.

What it contains

Make, model, body type, colour options, engine specifications, ADR compliance data, and high-performance vehicle details (power output, power-to-weight ratio). Each record corresponds to a vehicle variant sold in Australia.

Example uses

Registration validation against manufacturer records, vehicle specification lookups for insurance and fleet management, ADR compliance checking, and cross-referencing with VIN databases.

Terms of use

  • Data sourced from FCAI/VFACTS manufacturer submissions
  • Updated periodically as manufacturers release new models or variants
  • Data provided 'as is' — represents manufacturer specifications at time of supply
  • Not a substitute for registered vehicle records for compliance purposes

How data flows

  1. Vehicle manufacturers send specification information to FCAI (e.g. make, model, colour, weight).
  2. FCAI (now S&P Global) generates FCAI or HPV files and emails them to NEVDIS as text files.
  3. NEVDIS Data Team loads and validates the file against existing records.
  4. NEVDIS emails the output confirmation file back to FCAI (S&P Global).
