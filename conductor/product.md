# Product Definition — Austroads Data Info Hub

The **Austroads Data Info Hub** is a centralized internal portal for discovering, understanding, and requesting access to national data products across the **NEVDIS** and **TCA** programs.

## Vision

To provide a seamless, AI-powered "front door" for Austroads staff to access high-quality national data, reducing friction in data discovery and improving the efficiency of data-driven decision making.

## Core Value Proposition

- **Single Point of Discovery**: 20+ pages covering all major national data programs.
- **AI-Powered Assistance**: A multi-agent router system providing instant, factual answers from a Dataverse knowledge base.
- **Structured Metadata**: Consistent product definitions, field-level detail, and access instructions.

## Key Personas

1.  **Policy Analysts**: Need to know what data exists for vehicle registration or heavy vehicle telematics.
2.  **Data Scientists**: Need field-level technical specifications and update frequencies.
3.  **Program Managers**: Need a high-level overview of product availability and delivery methods.
