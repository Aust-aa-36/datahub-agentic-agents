Transport Certification Australia

TCA

Transport Certification Australia delivers data services built on the National Telematics Framework — supporting heavy vehicle compliance, road analytics, and access management for regulators and industry.

Data Elements

Foundation

The core telematics and spatial data components captured within TCA's National Telematics Framework — raw inputs that underpin all analytical products.

📋
Vehicle Enrolment Data (EMS)
Core registration and identity data for heavy vehicles enrolled in the NTF — linking vehicles, telematics devices, jurisdictions, and operators.
Heavy VehicleEMSEnrolment
📡
Spatiotemporal Data (Position & Speed)
GNSS telemetry capturing vehicle movement, speed, and positional accuracy at the trip level for operational and compliance analysis.
GNSSTelematicsSpeed
⚖️
Heavy Vehicle Mass Information Coming soon
Vehicle weight data from sensors or declared values — the raw mass readings that underpin infrastructure safety and compliance analysis.
MassSafetyInfrastructure
🛣️
Road Network Access & Aggregated Data Coming soon
Telematics-matched road-segment records providing counts, speeds, and mass metrics at the network level.
Road NetworkAggregated
🗺️
Spatial Data Services (IAC / B2B)
Standardised spatial datasets derived from Geoscape layers — defining jurisdictional access rules and constraints for IAC and B2B systems.
IACB2BGeoscape
